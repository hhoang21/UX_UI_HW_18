Hang is a beginner freelance UX/UI designer. Her background in Psychology has lead her down the path towards UX/UI design because of her passion for art and psychology. Her passions inspires her to design to solve problems users face. She is currently pursuing a certification at Georgia Institute of Technology Bootcamp for UX/UI Design.

My UX Skills

User Research

When approaching user research, I like to gain a solid foundation on what is already out there and what pain points users are currently facing. My preferred methods of research are surfing the web, conducting interviews and user test.

User Empathy

Because of my many years in customer service, I am able to step into the shoes of my user to understand my user through their eyes. To represent the pain points of my users, I use Miro to map out how my users feel and how to make them more at ease with each design decisions I plan to make.

Qualitative Interviews

When performing qualitative interviews, I am able to pay attention to their tone of voice and body language to understand what questions need to be pivoted and which questions I need to dive deeper into.
